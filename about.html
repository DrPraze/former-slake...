About Us

The internet is the most important tool for communication in the world. In the African society, there is a huge gap between most people and the internet. Although, some of it is as a result of lack of knowledge of the technology, most of the gap is as a result of the lack of affordable and accessible internet connections. People that don't have affordable and accessible internet connections at their grasp can be people who can't afford it or people in unfortunate positions with bad internet service or both. These people can definitely utilize the use of the internet to do wonderful things, but are currently limitled.

Slakenet fills the gap with a web 3.0 powered tech bridge that allows people to connect to the internet with ease making it 10 times more accessible and over 3 times cheaper than cellular networks. enabling users that still can't afford it to do simple tasks like watch ads, take surveys, play games, invite friends, mine etc. and earn slakecoins (The cryptocurrency that manages slakenet transactions) which they can use to purchase data plans and also earn money.
