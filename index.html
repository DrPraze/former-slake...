About Us

Slakenet is a platform that bridges the digital divide between people and the internet. We allow people to connect to the internet with ease with an aim to make it more affordable and accessible. We enable users that cannot afford to purchase data to do simple tasks and earn data with secure communication and technological systems powered by web 3.0 technology.
